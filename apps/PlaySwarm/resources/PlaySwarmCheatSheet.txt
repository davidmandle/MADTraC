Keyboard Shortcuts (only work when display window has focus, 
and are case sensitive):

  p - Pause/Unpause
  P - Display preferences
  space/right arrow - Move forward one time step
  backspace/delete/left arrow - Move backward one time step
  r - Reset to beginning of file
  o - Toggle occlusion calculation and display*
  t - Toggle trajectory tail display
  z - Toggle zoom cursor (draw a rectangle to zoom)
  a - Toggle "auto-zoom" - Experimental!
  Z - Reset to original zoom
  q - Quit

Mouse Behavior:

 Right Click on an agent to display preferences for that agent (size and color)

 When in zoom mode, draw a rectangle to zoom to that rectangle.  
 The rectangle is automatically adjusted to fit the aspect ratio 
 of the window.
